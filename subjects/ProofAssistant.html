Prototype of a new proof assistant in the Wolfram Language

It is well known that lambda-calculus is the mathematical model of sequential calculations. An equally simple formal system, known as pi-calculus, plays the same role in the framework of concurrent computations. In this project, a proof assistant for pi-calculus is developed within the framework of the Wolfram Language. This prototype includes a translator from the Wolfram language to the conventional proof assistant Isabelle/HOL and a function to translate the same code as a human-readable proof in LaTex.
